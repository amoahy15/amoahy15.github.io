Wake Forest University

  • Wake Forest University is a private research university in Winston-Salem, North Carolina. Founded in 1834, the university received its name from its original location in Wake Forest, north of Raleigh, North Carolina.

  • Founded in 1834, Wake Forest University is a private university located in Winston-Salem, N.C., with more than 8,000 students. The undergraduate population of more than 5,400 hails from 47 states and more than 45 foreign countries.

  • It has a total undergraduate enrollment of 5,441 (fall 2020), its setting is suburban, and the campus size is 340 acres. Wake Forest University's ranking in the 2022 edition of Best Colleges is National Universities, #28.

  • Wake Forest University is in the top 10% of the country for Computer Science. More specifically it was ranked #47 out of 502 schools by College Factual. It is also ranked #3 in the state of North Carolina.
