About Me

Humphrey Amoakohene


A Man and his Laptop

Born and raised in the Bronx, I have always had a connection with technology. Whether it was the countless hours I spent playing video games or browsing the Internet, I knew this was something I wanted to pursue in the future. Currently, I am an undergraduate student at Wake Forest University pursuing my Bachelor of Science in Computer Science and Mathematics with a minor in Entrepreneurship. In my first year at Wake Forest, I worked as a research assistant for Wake Forest's Intelligent Remote Sensing in Conservation & Discovery Group (IRSC Lab). During this experience, I applied training in Python, Pandas, Seaborn, SkLearn, and Machine Learning algorithms to analyze sample datasets of Covid-19 to detect variables that connected a high level of CDC exposure to variables like population and GDP.
